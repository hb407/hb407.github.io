My goal is to develop theoretical and computational methods to accurately predict electronic properties in molecules with competing electronic configurations.

My work investigates the mathematical properties of electronic structure methods, allowing us to identify novel ways of simulating processes such as chemical reaction pathways or light-induced electron excitations.

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Cambridge, UK.
